So how do you do it?

We are dedicated to showing you how to get your car to that special mark.

Here are just a few ways to get started:

Start driving. Now. Drop everything you're doing and go and start driving your car.

But I'm busy doing something-

That attitude is not going to help you reach your goal.

Keep driving until you reach your goal. Don't stop. Need to go to the bathroom? Too bad! Need to go to sleep? Too bad! Need to get gas? Get gas then keep going!

My car is a Tesla, I don't need gas-

I don't trust Elon Musk to make a car that lasts 200,000 miles. Go ahead and give up.

Car breaks down before you make it to 200,000? It wasn't meant to be. Get a different car that can handle it and try again.
